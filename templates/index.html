Why patronage?

For artists:

You like to create music, paint, write, organize events. But today everybody hopes, that you will make profit from it. That is not always possible. If you find yourself a patron, you don't have to be commercially successful from the start - patron's duty is to make sure, that you have everything you need, to do your favorite job. Only thing you have to do, is to be grateful for patron's support for you.

For patrons:

It may look, that being patron is a bad idea, because you spend money, but don't always get them back. But think about respect from society! People love books, artists, musicians, concerts, exhibitions. And if they see, that YOU are behind them, you will get better reputation. This is like commercial, but for longer term.
